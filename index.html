From the entrepreneurs who brought you Appfuel

In late 2011, Alfred Kam, Rex Kim, and Andrew Boos met at a brewery in Montreal to talk about a tech idea. Alfred and Andrew had been competitive swimmers together for a decade and had later met Rex at McGill University, where they all studied. The idea discussed that night was comically flawed, but at least it got the ball rolling. That conversation led to an entrepreneurial journey that led through three years, three countries, several pivots, several rebrands, three separate fundraises, a few near-bankruptcies, hires, departures, highs, lows, and a rewarding acquisition into a larger company. While Alfred and Rex were busy integrating and launching Appfuel as a part of the Tune Marketing Console, Andrew was working on Skyport Systems’ emergence from stealth as the first business addition to their founding team.

This group is working on groundbreaking stuff, interested in groundbreaking stuff, and likes to write about groundbreaking stuff. Please enjoy & reach out if you think we would get along.
